Welcome to our Web Applications

Below are brief explanations of each functionality along with links to access them:

  • Fibonacci Series Generator:
    The Fibonacci Series Generator calculates the Fibonacci series based on the user's input. The Fibonacci series is a sequence of numbers where each number is the sum of the two preceding ones, typically starting with 0 and 1.
  • Marks Calculator:
    The Marks Calculator computes the total and average marks for five subjects. Users can input their marks for each subject, and the calculator will provide the total marks obtained and the average marks.
  • Circle Calculator:
    The Circle Calculator determines the circumference and area of a circle based on the input radius. Users input the radius of the circle, and the calculator will output the circumference and area of the circle using the standard formulas.
